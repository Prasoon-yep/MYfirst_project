Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on "Forgot password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the instructions.

Where can I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "Order History" section.

How do I contact support?

Fill out the form on the "Contact Us" page, and our team will get back to you within 24 hours.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I sign up for a cooking class?

You can sign up for our cooking classes by visiting the "Classes" page and selecting the class you are interested in.

What is the refund policy?

Refunds are available up to 24 hours before the class. Please contact us for assistance.

Can I access recorded lessons?

Yes, recorded lessons are available to enrolled students. Visit the "My Account" page to view your courses.
